Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our land price prediction system

Our prediction system requires the following inputs:

  • Village: The location/village where the land is situated
  • Area: Total area in square feet
  • Distance to City: Distance from the nearest city in kilometers
  • Road Access: Type of road access (Rural Road, City Road, or Highway)
  • Water Source: Available water source (Well, Borewell, River, or None)
  • Electricity: Whether electricity is available
  • Land Use: Intended use (Agricultural, Commercial, or Residential)
  • Soil Type: Type of soil (Clay, Sandy, Loamy, or Rocky)
  • Nearby Development: Level of nearby development (Low, Medium, or High)

Our machine learning model is trained on extensive datasets and provides reliable price estimates. However, accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Data quality and completeness of input parameters
  • Market conditions and regional variations
  • Recent changes in infrastructure or development

Note: Predictions should be used as guidance and estimates, not absolute valuations. We recommend consulting with real estate professionals for final decisions.
